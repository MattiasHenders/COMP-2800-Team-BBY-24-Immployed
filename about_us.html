About Us Page

Our application connects employers with employees who have recovered from COVID-19 and have developed an resistance to the virus, thus are able to work. With 2 million unemployment cases in April 2020, our website helps restart the economy by providing everyone a chance to work again. Our team have worked to create a safe and reliable way to start the economy once more.
